Interested in joining the team?

We are currently recruiting for Fall 2021, Winter 2022 and Fall 2022. We welcome students from any training background, including kinesiology, engineering, computer science, neuroscience and physiology. We are committed to equity, diversity and inclusion and believe diversity adds to the richness and innovation of the lab.

Current positions available in the lab:

  • MSc students
  • PhD students
  • Post-doctoral fellows

For MSc and PhDs, preference will be given to students with:

  • proven excellent written and oral communication skills in English
  • working knowledge in or established programming experience (e.g., Python, R and/or Matlab)
  • a minimum grade point average of A-
  • a "team player" mindset
  • an eagerness to immerse themselves in self-directed learning

Students interested in applying can email Dr. Al-Khazraji directly with their CV, unofficial transcript, and a letter highlighting:
  • research interest(s)
  • educational background and research experience (if available)
  • what aspects of the lab/research projects interest them
  • a time they had to overcome a challenge or solve a difficult problem

Due to the volume of emails, Dr. Al-Khazraji will contact the top candidates for an interview and the selected candidates will then be asked to apply to the McMaster University Department of Kinesiology Graduate Program.

For prospective postdoctoral fellows, please contact Dr. Al-Khazraji directly to discuss application to fellowship funding competitions.
